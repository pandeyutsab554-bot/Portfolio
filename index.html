Engineering Projects

Pyrolysis Reactor Design

Designed full reactor geometry in SolidWorks for high-temperature (~1000°C) biomass conversion. Applied heat transfer and fluid dynamics to optimize thermal distribution and gas flow. Selected high-temperature materials with insulation and pressure containment systems. Stress and thermal analysis validated design integrity across all operating conditions.

ATM Accessibility Device

Designed a linkage-based interface mechanism to improve ATM accessibility for disabled users. Developed and iterated CAD models through multiple design cycles. Fabricated and validated physical prototypes using 3D printing, confirming linkage performance against accessibility requirements.

Fusion Splicing — Optical Fiber Alignment

Precision optical fiber alignment and nanoscale splicing achieving ~0.01 dB fusion loss. Developed alignment protocols for sub-millimeter fiber geometries used in AFM cantilever actuation research.

Sparki Robot + Egg Mechanism

Developed passive geometry-based mechanism to reorient objects without active control. Integrated Arduino sensors and robotic system for real-time interaction. Demonstrated constraint-based mechanical design principles in a hands-on robotics context.

Research

AFM Parametric Sensing — NSF/NIH/USDA Funded (2025–Present)

Engineered precision Ag-coated optical fiber electrodes for AFM cantilever actuation, incorporating TiO₂ dielectric insulation layer achieving ~30% target reflectivity. Developed repeatable epoxy masking process enabling selective thin-film deposition on sub-millimeter fiber geometries. Executed chemical silver deposition (AgNO₃ + glucose reduction) producing uniform thin films. Investigated electrostatic coupling and parametric stiffness modulation (2f₀) for cantilever control, exploring noise squeezing to push force sensitivity beyond thermal noise limits.
